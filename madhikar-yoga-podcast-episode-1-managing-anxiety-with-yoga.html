Madhikar Yoga Podcast Episode 1: Managing Anxiety with Yoga

Written on Sep 25, 2024

Anxiety and Yoga

Welcome to the first episode of the Madhikar Yoga podcast. We are going to discuss various aspects of Yoga and how it can help us improve our lives.

This episode deals with problem that we all have experienced at many times in our lives. Anxiety is often crippling and can also affect us for prolonged periods of time.

This episode is divided in to two parts:

  • Part 1: A free-form discussion about anxiety. Here we learn about what the different types of anxiety are, how to recognise them, and some simple tricks to manage anxiety - or at the very least quickly reduce it in the moment. We also explore some theories of how anxiety is described from the Yogic context.

  • Part 2: Guided meditation. This is a recording of a short session of guided meditation and visualisation conducted by Dr. Prabhakar. We recommend listening to it several times.
